Ethnographic Mapping in the Coast Salish World

A project by Brian Thom, Department of Anthropology

These maps were scanned from a review of over 700 ethnographies written about Coast Salish peoples.

Browse thematically organized maps of the Coast Salish world drawn from ethnographies over the past 150 years.
